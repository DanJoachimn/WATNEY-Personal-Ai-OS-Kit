[AI_NAME] is live. Don't sit there wondering what to ask it — steal one of these. Each takes under a minute and works right now, day one, no extra setup.

## 1. Make it write like you

Say: *"Draft a reply to this — [paste a real email or message you owe someone]."*

You'll get a reply in YOUR voice — the one [AI_NAME] just spent twenty minutes learning — not robot voice. Send it or tweak it. Either way, that email is off your plate.

## 2. Empty your head

Say: *"Here's everything on my plate right now: [braindump — messy is fine]. Organize it and tell me what I'm forgetting."*

A two-minute ramble comes back as a clean list, sorted by what's urgent — usually with one thing you'd genuinely forgotten at the bottom.

## 3. Text it from the couch

Tonight, laptop closed, open Telegram and send a voice note: *"Remind me tomorrow at 9 to [the thing you keep forgetting]."*

The reminder arrives at 9. That's [AI_NAME] working while your Mac sleeps in another room. Get used to that feeling — it's the whole point.

## 4. Borrow its brain

Say: *"I'm weighing [a real decision you're facing — option A vs option B]. Lay out the trade-offs and tell me what you'd pick."*

You'll get a straight recommendation with reasons — not a wishy-washy "it depends." Disagree with it? Say so. It argues back politely, and the thinking sharpens both of you.

## 5. Ask what happens while you sleep

Say: *"What will you do for me tonight and tomorrow morning?"*

[AI_NAME] will walk you through its overnight routine — the quiet work that happens at 2 AM, and the brief being prepared for your morning. Then tomorrow proves it.
