## Why this matters for the AI

When [PARTNER_NAME] says *"the supplier sent something weird, draft a reply,"* the AI reads `Companies/[supplier name].md`. It now knows: who they are, the relationship, lead times, past quirks, who to address the email to. Better drafts on first try.

1. Copy `_Company template.md` and rename (e.g. `Acme Manufacturing.md`).
2. Fill in.
3. Tell the AI: *"new company — [name] — read their file."*
