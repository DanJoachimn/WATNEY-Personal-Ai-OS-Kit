# Amphetamine — reach [AI_NAME] with the lid closed (late add-on)

**Value:** text [AI_NAME] while your laptop's shut and you're away from your desk — it stays reachable.

**How it works, simply:** Amphetamine keeps the Mac awake, tied to *plugged in*. On power → awake + reachable, lid open or shut. On battery → sleeps normally. So a closed laptop in a bag just sleeps — no overheating, battery saved.

*(Skip this if you dock to an external monitor — that's clamshell, automatic and free.)*

## Setup (guided manual — Amphetamine is a menu-bar app the AI can't click for you)

1. **Install Amphetamine** (free): **https://apps.apple.com/app/amphetamine/id937984704** → click **Get**, Touch ID.
2. **Preferences → Session Defaults:** uncheck *"Closed-Display Mode: Allow system sleep when display is closed."* Default Duration → Indefinitely.
3. **General tab:** check *"Launch at login"*; uncheck *"Start session when Amphetamine launches"* and *"Start session after waking from sleep."*
4. **Triggers → ➕:** add criterion *"power adapter is connected"* (only — no battery %); in its options, uncheck *"Allow system sleep when display is closed."* Save.

**Done:** plugged in anywhere → reachable lid-closed. Unplugged → asleep.
